## 📋 The Methodology: "Test $\rightarrow$ Stabilize $\rightarrow$ Document"
Every project in my portfolio follows a rigorous field-engineering process:
1. **Stress Test:** Pushing the tool to its limits to identify "silent failures" and documentation gaps.
2. **Stabilization:** Building the necessary wrappers, auth-chains, and governance rules.
3. **Runbook Creation:** Documenting not only the solution but the *failed hypotheses* to accelerate others' learning.
4. **Public Release:** Sharing the knowledge as a reproducible field note.
